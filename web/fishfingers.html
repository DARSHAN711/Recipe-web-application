HOW TO MAKE FISH FINGERS

Ingredients for Fish Fingers

*)Boneless fish fillets 2
*)Salt to taste
*)Lemon juice 2 tablespoons
*)Egg 1
*)Mustard sauce 1 tablespoon
*)Refined flour 1/2 tablespoon
*)Medium onion finely chopped 1
*)Black olives finely chopped 1 tablespoon
*)Fresh parsley finely chopped 1 tablespoon
*)Black peppercorns crushed to taste
*)Mayonnaise 2 tablespoons
*)Dried breadcrumbs for coating

Method

Step 1:
Cut fish fillets into thick strips and put in a bowl. Add salt, lemon juice, egg, mustard sauce and flour and mix well.
Step 2:
To make the dip, mix together onions, olives, parsley, crushed peppercorns, salt and mayonnaise in another bowl. Transfer the dip into a serving bowl.
Step 3:
Heat sufficient oil in a deep pan.
Step 4:
Spread breadcrumbs on a plate. Coat the fish pieces with it, gently slide into hot oil and deep-fry till golden brown and crisp. Drain on absorbent paper.
Step 5:
Transfer them into a serving bowl and serve hot with the prepared
