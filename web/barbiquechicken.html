HOW TO MAKE BARBIQUE CHICKEN

Ingredients for Barbecue Chicken

*)Barbeque sauce ¼ cup
*)Chicken wings tips removed 8
*)Oil 1 tablespoon + to deep fry
*)Salt to taste
*)Crushed black peppercorns to taste
*)Refined flour (maida) 2 tablespoons
*)Garlic chopped 1 tablespoon
*)Spring onion greens chopped for garnishing

Method

Step 1:
Heat sufficient oil in a pan.
Step 2:
Take chicken in a bowl. Add salt, crushed peppercorns and flour and mix well.
Step 3:
Deep-fry marinated chicken in hot oil till golden and crisp. Drain on absorbent paper.
Step 4:
Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a non-stick pan. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds. Add barbecue sauce and mix. Add fried chicken, mix and cook for 1 minute.
Step 5:
Serve hot garnished with spring onion greens.
