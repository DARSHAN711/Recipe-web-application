HOW TO MAKE DEVILED EGGS >

Ingredients for Guacamole Devilled Eggs

*)Medium avocado 1
*)Eggs boiled 4
*)Small tomato finely chopped 1
*)Small onion finely chopped 1
*)Green chillies finely chopped 1-2
*)Lemon juice 2 teaspoons
*)Fresh coriander leaves chopped 1 tablespoon
*)Salt to taste
*)Fresh parsley sprig to garnish

Method

Step 1:
Halve the eggs. Remove the yolks and put into a bowl and place the egg white shells on a serving platter.
Step 2:
Halve, seed the avocado and scoop out the flesh and add to the same bowl. Add tomato, onion, green chillies, lemon juice, coriander leaves and salt and mash well with a fork into smooth paste to make. Step 3:
Fill guacamole into a piping bag fitted with a star nozzle and pipe into egg white shells.
Step 4:
Serve garnished with parsley sprigs.
