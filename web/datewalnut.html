HOW TO MAKE DATE WALNUT

Ingredients

Seedless dates chopped 1/4 cup + to sprinkle
Walnuts chopped 1/4 cup + to sprinkle
Butter 1 cup
Castor sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 3
Vanilla essence 2 teaspoon
Refined flour 1 cup + 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 1 teaspoon
Salt to taste
Milk 1/4 cup
Icing sugar for dusting

Method

Step 1:
Preheat an oven to180ยบ C. Grease a loaf tin, sprinkle some dry flour all over and remove excess flour.
Step 2:
Take butter and sugar in a mixing bowl and beat with a hand blender till light and fluffy.
Step 3:
Break in 1 egg at a time and beat till well incorporated.
Step 4:
Add vanilla essence and beat again. Sieve together refined flour, baking powder and salt into the bowl and fold in with a spatula.
Step 5:
Take walnuts and dates in another bowl, add 2 tablespoons flour and mix well.
Step 6:
Add this mixture to the batter and fold in. Add milk and mix well to form a lump free batter.
Step 7:
Pour the batter into the prepared loaf tin and gently tap it on the worktop to level the surface. Sprinkle some dates and walnuts on top.
Step 8:
Keep the loaf tin in the preheated oven and bake for 35-40 minutes.
Step 9:
Take the tin out of the oven and let it cool down to room temperature. Slice, arrange the slices on a serving platter, dust some icing sugar on top and serve.
