HOW TO MAKE FOOD COCKTAIL

Ingredients

*)Fruit cocktail 1 tin
*)Orange 1
*)Vanilla custard 1 cup
*)Fresh cream 2-3 tablespoons
*)Orange squash 2 tablespoons
*)Brown sugar 3 tablespoons
*)Cinnamon powder 1/4 teaspoon

Method

Step 1:
Grate the rind of the orange into a mixing bowl. Add custard, fresh cream and orange squash and beat with an electric beater.
Step 2:
Strain the fruit cocktail and add to the custard mixture. Add fruit cocktail and mix well.
Step 3:
Half fill ramekin moulds with this fruit mixture.
Step 4:
Mix brown sugar and cinnamon powder in a small bowl. Step 5:
Top the fruit-custard mixture with brown sugar mixture and refrigerate for 2-3 hours.
Step 6:
Serve chilled.
