HOW TO MAKE FISH MOILEE

Ingredients for Fish Moilee

*)Pomfrets cut into 1 inch thick darnes 500 grams
*)Salt to taste
*)Turmeric powder 1/2 teaspoon
*)Lemon juice 1 teaspoon
*)Coconut oil 1 glass
*)Mustard seeds 1 teaspoon
*)Curry leaves 12-16
*)Large onion finely chopped 1
*)Ginger-garlic paste 1 tablespoon
*)Green chillies slit 2
*)Coconut milk 1 cup

Method

Step 1:
Take pomfret darnes in a bowl, add salt, ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder and lemon juice and mix well.
Step 2:
Heat coconut oil in a non-stick pan add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves and onions, mix and sauté till fragrant.
Step 3:
Add ginger-garlic paste, mix and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Add remaining turmeric powder and mix well.
Step 4:
Add green chillies, 2 tablespoons water and the marinated fish and mix well.
Step 5:
Add salt and coconut milk, mix well and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
Step 6:
Transfer into a serving bowl and serve warm.
