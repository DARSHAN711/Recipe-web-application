HOW TO MAKE SITAPHAL CREAM

Ingredients

*)Fresh custard apple (sitaphal) pulp
*)Large custard apple seeded 1 + For topping
*)Vanilla ice cream 3 scoops
*)Sugar powdered 1 tablespoon
*)Fresh cream 1/4 cup
*)Green cardamom powder 1/4 teaspoon
*)Whipped cream For topping
*)Fresh mint sprigs to garnish

Method

Step 1:
Take ice cream in a mixing bowl, add custard apple puree, powdered sugar, cream, custard apple flesh and cardamom powder and whisk well.
Step 2:
Put the mixture into individual serving glasses, top with some custard apple flesh, whipped cream rosette and garnish with mint sprigs.
Step 3:
Serve chilled.
