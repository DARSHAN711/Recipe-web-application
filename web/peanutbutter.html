HOW TO MAKE PEANUT BUTTER

Ingredients

*)Peanut butter 2 tablespoons
*)Chocolate brownies 4
*)Vanilla icecream 10 scoops
*)Honey 2 tablespoons

Method

Step 1:
Put honey and peanut butter into a bowl and beat well with an electric beater. Add 8 scoops of vanilla ice cream and beat again. Roughly crumble brownies and add to the bowl and continue to beat taking care that some brownie pieces remain.
Step 2:
Transfer the mixture into a silicon mould.
Step 3:
Put 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream in another bowl and beat well. Spoon dollops of this ice cream over the peanut-vanilla mixture in the mould and create marble effects with a spoon.
Step 4:
Cover the silicon mould with a lid and refrigerate till frozen.
Step 5:
Serve chilled.
